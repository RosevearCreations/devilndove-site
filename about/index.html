About

We make things to make days better.

Devil n Dove is our workshop, our creative outlet, and our way of keeping life moving—especially on the days that don’t cooperate. We’re not chasing perfection. We’re chasing progress, curiosity, and the kind of making that feels like breathing again.

What we do

  • Jewelry, mixed media, and small-batch creations
  • 3D design, printing, CNC, carving, and casting experiments
  • Laser engraving, sublimation, and “what else can we try?” projects
  • Behind-the-scenes videos: the wins, the fails, and the laughs

Why we do it

Making is therapy here. It’s structure, focus, and a reason to show up. We work around real limitations—and we share that honestly—because it’s part of the story and part of the strength.

If you’ve ever used creativity to stay grounded, you’ll get it. If not—welcome anyway. Pull up a chair.
